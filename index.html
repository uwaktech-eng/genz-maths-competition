Competition Rules & Penalties

📋 Competition Rules

  • ✓All participants must provide accurate and truthful information during registration.
  • ✓Participants must be within the specified age range and attend a registered school.
  • ✓During online exams, participants must work independently without external help or resources.
  • ✓Participants must maintain ethical conduct throughout the competition.
  • ✓All decisions made by the organizing committee are final and binding.
  • ✓Participants must comply with all instructions given by invigilators and organizers.

⚠️ Penalties for Violations

  • ✗Providing False Information: Immediate disqualification and entry fee forfeiture.
  • ✗Examination Malpractice: Disqualification from current and future competitions + blacklist status.
  • ✗Unauthorized Assistance: Automatic disqualification and removal from leaderboard.
  • ✗Disruptive Behavior: Warning first offense, suspension for repeat violations.
  • ✗Plagiarism/Copying: All involved participants disqualified with no refund.
  • ✗Violation of Terms: Disqualification and report to participant's school.

Note: By registering and submitting this form, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to abide by all competition rules and penalties listed above. Violation of any rule may result in immediate disqualification.
